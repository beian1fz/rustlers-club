Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Membership Requirements

Rustlers Club is a private, members-only social club operating in accordance with Texas statutes. To become a member:

  • You must be 21 years of age or older
  • You must present a valid government-issued ID
  • You must pay the $10 daily membership fee
  • You must agree to abide by all club rules and regulations

2. Club Operations

Rustlers Club operates as a private social gaming club under Texas law. Important operational notes:

  • We charge NO TIME CHARGES on any games
  • All games are member-vs-member or member-vs-pool
  • The club does not profit from the games at play
  • Digital point contributions support the members' pool
  • All gaming is conducted with transparency and fairness

3. Member Conduct

All members are expected to maintain proper conduct while at Rustlers Club:

  • Treat all members and staff with respect
  • Follow all gaming rules and procedures
  • No cheating, collusion, or unfair play
  • No disruptive or violent behavior
  • Management reserves the right to refuse service or revoke membership

4. Gaming Rules

All gaming at Rustlers Club follows established rules:

  • Standard rules apply for poker games (Hold'em, Omaha)
  • Custom games (TX Bullseye, Ultimate San Antonio Hold'em, etc.) have specific rules available at the club
  • Tournament rules are posted for each event
  • Floor decisions are final
  • Members must follow proper gaming etiquette

5. Amenities & Services

As a member, you have access to:

  • Complimentary food and beverages (soft drinks, beer, snacks)
  • Multiple TVs for sports viewing
  • Professional dealers and staff
  • Clean, secure facility
  • Bar service (alcohol coming soon, pending license)

6. Privacy & Security

Rustlers Club takes your privacy and security seriously:

  • Member information is kept confidential
  • Security cameras monitor all gaming areas
  • Licensed security personnel on premises
  • All financial transactions are secure

7. Charitable Contributions

A percentage of all membership fees goes directly to local San Antonio charities. Members have the opportunity to vote on which organizations receive support.

8. Disclaimer

Rustlers Club is a private, members-only social club operating within Texas statutes. Our games are designed to ensure fairness, transparency, elements of skill, and a member-vs-member playing experience. The club does not profit from the games at play, fostering a unique and engaging environment for all members.
