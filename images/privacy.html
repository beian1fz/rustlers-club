Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Rustlers Club ("we," "our," or "us") respects the privacy of our members and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our facility or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, government-issued ID information
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address (if provided)
  • Membership Information: Membership status, play history, point balance
  • Security Information: CCTV footage for security purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Verify age and identity for membership eligibility
  • Manage your membership and player account
  • Track gaming activity and point balances
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Communicate about tournaments, promotions, and club updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Rustlers Club, our members, or others
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure storage of member records
  • Limited access to personal information (authorized personnel only)
  • Security cameras monitoring gaming areas
  • Regular security assessments

Your Rights

As a member, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)

Cardopz Platform

Our digital services are provided through Cardopz (rustlersclub.cardopz.com). When using these services, you are also subject to Cardopz's privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

Age Restriction

Rustlers Club is strictly 21+. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from individuals under 21 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify members of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy at our facility and on our website.
